BRAN THE HERO'S LIFE PLAN

The Black Emperor… One of the 8 rank 0 adventurers in the world. Each rank 0 adventurer has power equivalent to war weapons and can be considered human weapons! The Black Emperor fended off the causer of the tragedy, the Black Dragon’s attack, and covered the entire city in a downpour of healing rain. As if everyone heard the voice of the commander, they all simultaneously looked up into the sky and had a sparkle in their eyes. Then, as if answering their expectations, the Black Emperor faced the Black Dragon and threw out his hand. “Divine Destruction Spear” An ominous black spear appeared from his outstretched hand. Every attribute began to mix and compress as much as possible within the spear that appeared. The mass of magic was morphed into a spear and blasted off. Bran doesn’t think hunting monsters is particularly important for society. So he decides to quit being the Black Emperor and turns to something a little more important. Which is completing odd jobs that no one else wants to do!
